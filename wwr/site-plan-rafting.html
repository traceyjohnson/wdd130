Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to convince visitors that they should choose our company over our competitors and that whitewater rafting is right for them. The website should help gain customers and generate revenue.

Audience

This site's intended audience is adventureous people visiting or living near the Dry Oar River area who are looking for something exciting to do. They are anticipated to have disposable income, and a love of the great outdoors and a need for adventure. Visitors to the site, and those who will most be able to decide to book a trip, will likely be between the ages of 16-55. Our loyal customer base consists of those living in Dry Oar River City, with some scattering of beach house communities on the shoreline of Dry Oar River. Most potential customers will be accessing the site on their phones, however there will be a need for the website to be desktop and tablet compatible.
